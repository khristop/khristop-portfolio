UI/UX Designer, Design Lead

Bewear

Description

Bewear is a web application that suggests clothes based on the weather and your temperature sensitivity, which will calculate your sensitivity to hot and cold temperatures and show the best outfits for you.

Background

Bewear project is a project accomplished while studying at Langara College. The team was composed of 7 students, 3 designers and 4 developers. I was a UI/UX designer and Designer Lead. As a UI/UX designer, I created wireframes, mockups, prototypes and UX research. As a Designer Lead, I managed the team, assigned tasks, and supervised the project's progress.

Process

I helped the team define the project's scope and the features we were going to implement. After defining the project goals, I worked with the design team on creating user personas, wireframes, assets and mockups. I also worked with the development team to ensure the design was implemented correctly.
