Description

DailyGo is part of the Fitness/HealthTech industry. The app strives to address the pain point of motivation and engagement in our everyday walking routine. Many individuals struggle to maintain a consistent walking habit and often get bored or lose motivation. This app aims to address these challenges by providing an interactive and gamified walking experience, incorporating elements of collaboration and handling our inner monsters.

https://www.dailygo.ca
Background

DailyGo project is the capstone project accomplished while studying at Langara College. The team was composed for 6 students, 3 designers and 3 developers. I was a UI/UX designer. As a designer, I was in charge of creating wireframes, mockups, prototypes and marketing assets. I also helped the developers with the development of the marketing website.

Design Process

I helped the team to define the scope of the project and the features that we were going to implement. I also helped to define the user personas and the user journey. After that, I started to work on the wireframes and the mockups for the challenges feature. I created the wireframes and mockups using Figma. I also created the marketing website using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Design Tools

In this project, we had to create videos, assets and animations for the applicaion. So, we made use of the Adobe suite and Figma to create all the assets.
